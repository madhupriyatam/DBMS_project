HTH is an voluntary and non-governmental organisation.It maintains Online library of organ and blood donars in India. Sometimes Doctors and Hospitals have to face the dificulty in finding the blood group Donors and organ donars at right time. HTH has attempted to provide the answer by taking upon itself the task of collecting blood donars nationwise for the cause and care of people in need.At any point of time the people who are in need can reach the donars through our search facility. By mobilising people and organisation who desire to make a difference in the lives of people in need. On the basis of humanity, Everyone is welcome to register as a blood donar.
