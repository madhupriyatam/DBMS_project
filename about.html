About Us

Help The Helpless

HTH is a non-profitable organization which is started with an intention of solving the problem of ever growing requirements of blood, organs (liver, kidneys, eyes) due to accidents or organ failures. People who are willing to donate blood, organs can register here simply by sitting at home providing their blood group, contact no, address, health problems (if any). The information stored here is in such a way that it is accessible to all the blood banks, hospitals so that they can have ease of accessing to provide necessary medical help. Blood recipients can also make order of blood providing their basic details. It provides the information of various blood donation camps so that citizens can check the details in advance. So, with the advancement in technology one need not be omnivagant for help, all they need is to go through our website in which we hope to have a solution for all.
